# Banking Fees and Charges Schedule

## Account Maintenance Fees

| Account Type | Monthly Fee | Waiver Condition |
|---|---|---|
| Regular Savings | 10,000 VND | Balance > 1M VND |
| Premium Savings | Free | Always free |
| Checking Account | 20,000 VND | Salary account |
| Student Account | Free | Valid student ID |

## Transfer Fees

### Domestic Transfers
- Same bank: Free
- Interbank (NAPAS) up to 500M VND: 11,000 VND
- Interbank (NAPAS) 500M - 1B VND: 22,000 VND
- Urgent transfer (within 30 minutes): additional 33,000 VND

### International Transfers
- SWIFT outgoing: 250,000 VND + 0.05% (min 200K, max 2M VND)
- SWIFT incoming: 100,000 VND flat
- Telex transfer: 300,000 VND + 0.05%

## Card Fees

### Debit Card
- Card issuance: Free (first card)
- Card replacement: 100,000 VND
- Annual fee: 50,000 VND (waived first year)
- ATM withdrawal (own bank): Free
- ATM withdrawal (other bank): 3,300 VND
- ATM withdrawal (international): 50,000 VND + 2%

### Credit Card Fees
- Standard annual fee: 300,000 VND
- Premium annual fee: 1,500,000 VND
- Late payment penalty: 4% of minimum payment
- Cash advance: 3% (min 50,000 VND)
- Foreign transaction fee: 2.5%
- Over-limit fee: 200,000 VND
- Card replacement: 150,000 VND

## Loan Fees

### Processing Fees
- Personal loan processing: 1% of loan amount (min 500K VND)
- Mortgage processing: 0.5% of loan amount (min 1M VND)
- Business loan processing: 0.3% of loan amount (min 2M VND)

### Early Repayment Fees
- Within first year: 3% of remaining balance
- After first year: 2% of remaining balance
- After third year: 1% of remaining balance
- After fifth year: Free

## Special Services

- Account statement (paper): 20,000 VND per copy
- Account statement (email): Free
- Balance confirmation letter: 100,000 VND
- Notarized copies: 50,000 VND each
- Account freeze/unfreeze: Free (fraud-related), 100,000 VND (customer request)
