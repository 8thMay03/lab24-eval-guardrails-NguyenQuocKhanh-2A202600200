# Banking Products & Services Guide

## Savings Accounts

### Regular Savings Account
A regular savings account offers a competitive interest rate of 4.5% per annum for balances above 10 million VND. The minimum opening balance is 500,000 VND. Interest is calculated daily and paid monthly. Customers can make unlimited withdrawals but must maintain the minimum balance.

### Fixed Deposit Account
Fixed deposits offer higher interest rates ranging from 5.0% to 7.5% per annum depending on the term. Available terms include 1, 3, 6, 12, 18, and 24 months. Early withdrawal results in a penalty rate of 0.5% per annum. The minimum deposit is 10 million VND for standard terms and 50 million VND for premium rates.

### Online Savings Account
The online savings account provides a 0.3% bonus interest rate compared to regular savings. Available exclusively through the mobile banking app and internet banking platform. No minimum balance requirement and no monthly maintenance fees.

## Loan Products

### Personal Loans
Personal loans are available for amounts from 10 million to 500 million VND. Interest rates start at 8.5% per annum for secured loans and 12% for unsecured loans. Loan terms range from 6 months to 60 months. Required documents include proof of income, identification (CCCD), and proof of residence.

### Home Mortgage Loans
Mortgage loans cover up to 70% of property value with interest rates from 7.5% to 9.5% per annum. The maximum loan term is 25 years. Borrowers must have a stable income of at least 3 times the monthly repayment amount. Property appraisal is conducted by the bank at no extra cost for the first assessment.

### Business Loans
Business loans for SMEs range from 100 million to 10 billion VND. Collateral requirements vary: secured loans require 120% collateral coverage, while credit-based loans are available for existing customers with good track records. The approval process takes 5-7 business days for secured loans and 10-15 days for credit-based loans.

## Credit Cards

### Standard Credit Card
The standard credit card has an annual fee of 300,000 VND with a credit limit from 10 million to 100 million VND. The interest-free period is 45 days for full payment. Cash advance fee is 3% of the transaction amount with a minimum of 50,000 VND. Late payment penalty is 4% of the minimum payment amount.

### Premium Credit Card
The premium credit card offers a credit limit from 100 million to 500 million VND. Annual fee is 1,500,000 VND, waived if annual spending exceeds 200 million VND. Benefits include airport lounge access, travel insurance up to 2 billion VND, and 2x reward points on dining and travel purchases.

### Corporate Credit Card
Corporate credit cards are issued to businesses with a minimum annual revenue of 5 billion VND. Features include consolidated billing, expense tracking, individual spending limits per employee, and integration with major accounting software.

## Digital Banking Services

### Internet Banking
Internet banking supports balance inquiry, fund transfers up to 500 million VND per transaction, bill payments, and account management. Two-factor authentication (2FA) via OTP is mandatory for all transactions. Daily transfer limit can be customized from 10 million to 2 billion VND.

### Mobile Banking App
The mobile banking app supports biometric login (fingerprint and face recognition), QR code payments, mobile top-up, and instant notifications. The app is available on iOS 14+ and Android 10+. Peer-to-peer transfers are instant and free for amounts under 10 million VND.

### ATM Network
The bank operates 2,500 ATMs nationwide. Daily withdrawal limit is 50 million VND per card. Interbank ATM withdrawals incur a fee of 3,300 VND per transaction. Cardless withdrawal via QR code is available at all bank-owned ATMs.

## Wire Transfers

### Domestic Transfers
Domestic bank transfers are processed within 1-2 hours during business hours (8:00 AM to 5:00 PM). Same-bank transfers are instant and free. Interbank transfers via NAPAS cost 11,000 VND for amounts up to 500 million VND.

### International Wire Transfers
International transfers support SWIFT and Telex with processing time of 1-3 business days. Fees include a flat fee of 250,000 VND plus 0.05% of the transfer amount (minimum 200,000 VND, maximum 2 million VND). Available currencies include USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, SGD, and CNY.

## Account Management

### Account Opening Requirements
To open an account, customers must be at least 18 years old and present a valid CCCD (Citizen Identity Card) or passport. For foreign nationals, a valid visa or residence card is required in addition to a passport. The account opening process takes approximately 15-30 minutes at any branch.

### KYC and Compliance
All accounts are subject to Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements. Enhanced due diligence (EDD) applies to politically exposed persons (PEPs) and high-risk customers. Suspicious transaction reports (STR) are filed for transactions exceeding 300 million VND or any unusual patterns.

### Account Closure
Account closure requires a written request and valid identification. Outstanding loans and credit card balances must be settled before closure. Remaining balance is transferred to a designated account or paid by cashier's check. Account closure is processed within 5 business days.

## Security Policies

### Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 8 characters long, containing uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Passwords expire every 90 days and cannot be reused for 12 cycles. Account lockout occurs after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.

### Fraud Prevention
The bank employs real-time transaction monitoring using AI-based anomaly detection. Suspicious transactions trigger automatic alerts via SMS and email. Customers can instantly freeze their cards through the mobile app. All online transactions require OTP verification for amounts exceeding 5 million VND.

### Data Protection
Customer data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Data retention period is 10 years after account closure as required by Vietnamese banking regulations. The bank complies with Circular 09/2020/TT-NHNN on information technology risk management.
